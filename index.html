Who we are

Free social tennis in Didsbury

About us

Community-run tennis organised by volunteers who give their time freely, supported by McrActive to provide accessible tennis for Didsbury and South Manchester. An inclusive, friendly environment where fun and social connection are at the heart of every session.

Sociable & inclusive

Warm, friendly atmosphere where everyone's welcome. Random partnering keeps things fresh and helps you meet new people.

Beautiful location

Set in the lovely surroundings of Fletcher Moss Park — a great way to exercise, unwind, and enjoy the outdoors.

No fees or contracts

No membership fees or contracts. Just a small contribution towards balls every few months to keep things running.

Your first session

Just turn up, introduce yourself and you'll be paired up straight away. We rotate partners throughout so you'll get to play with everyone. Bring water, wear trainers, and expect a good time.
